Quick-Reference Pocket Cards

Print this page. Keep it on your desk. Use it on every listing.

Card 1: The 5-Part Listing Structure

#SectionOne-Line Rule
1HookFirst 15 words decide if they keep reading. No "Welcome to."
2LifestyleSell the life, not the house. Paint a picture.
3Features (FBF)Every spec reaches Layer 3 — Feature → Benefit → Feeling.
4NeighborhoodSpecific beats generic. Walk Score, coffee shops, commute times.
5CTAConfident, not desperate. Assume they're already sold.

Card 2: The FBF Framework

LayerQuestionExample
1 — FeatureWhat is it?"Quartz countertops"
2 — BenefitSo what?"Never stains. Never seals."
3 — FeelingWhy does that matter?"Sunday mornings around the island, coffee in hand, mess forgotten."
